Welcome to Brisket Basics

Whether you're firing up a smoker for the first time or looking to sharpen your BBQ game, this site will guide you through every step of cooking a brisket—from selecting the right cut of meat to slicing and serving it like a pitmaster. Brisket might seem intimidating, but with the right prep and patience, it’s one of the most rewarding meats you can cook.

Here you’ll find a straightforward, no-nonsense approach to brisket. We’ll cover trimming and seasoning, go over cooking methods like smoking or oven-roasting, and show you how to slice and store your brisket for maximum tenderness and flavor. Everything’s broken into simple steps across three pages, so you can take it at your own pace and come back whenever you need a refresher.
